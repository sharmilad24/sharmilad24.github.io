Stop 01 — About

The short version

I like the full ride: pulling messy data in, cleaning and modeling it, training something that predicts, and putting a real interface on top that people can actually use. My favorite recent build is a MARTA trip planner that predicts rail delays using a model trained on live transit data I collected myself, partly because I was tired of guessing when trains would show up.

Outside of data work, I research navigation systems for blind and low vision users, tutor on campus, and ran Girls Who Code back at Denmark High School.
